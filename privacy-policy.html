Dungeon Hauler

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how xBeauGaming handles information for Dungeon Hauler.

Contact

Developer: xBeauGaming

Email: xbeaugaming@gmail.com

Information We Collect

Dungeon Hauler is designed to be playable without creating an account. The game stores gameplay progress locally on your device, such as save data, inventory, settings, and progression.

If you send feedback or support email, we receive the information you choose to include in that email, such as your message, email address, and any device details you provide.

The app may check a small public version file on this website to tell you when a newer Google Play build is available. This check does not send your save data, feedback text, or player profile to xBeauGaming.

The app may automatically send limited error diagnostics to xBeauGaming through a Google-hosted endpoint. A diagnostic can include a random installation identifier, app and build version, platform, broad device/build features, current screen, error category and message, and limited sanitized gameplay state. It is designed not to send your player name, save file, inventory, email address, credentials, or precise location.

Ads And Purchases

Dungeon Hauler includes Google Mobile Ads and Google's consent platform. Closed Test 1 requests only Google's clearly marked test ads; future production builds may serve live ads after the remaining consent and privacy-options checks are complete. Google may process device or advertising identifiers, app and ad interactions, diagnostics, IP-derived approximate location, and related information to provide, secure, measure, and improve advertising services.

Dungeon Hauler includes Google Play Billing for an optional one-time Remove Ads purchase. Purchase processing, localized pricing, purchase status, and restoration are handled by Google Play. xBeauGaming does not receive your full payment card details.

How Information Is Used

  • To save and restore game progress.
  • To respond to support requests and feedback.
  • To provide and measure ads where permitted and fulfill optional purchases.
  • To understand crashes, bugs, and gameplay issues through limited error diagnostics.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers used by the app, such as Google Play, Google AdMob, email providers, or hosting providers.

Children

Dungeon Hauler is intended for teens and adults. It is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can play without sending feedback.
  • You can decline optional rewarded ads and continue playing; failed or unavailable ads do not block normal navigation.
  • You can delete local game data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage.
  • You can contact xBeauGaming at xbeaugaming@gmail.com with privacy questions.

Changes

This policy may be updated as Dungeon Hauler changes. The effective date will be updated when the policy changes.
