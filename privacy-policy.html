Dungeon Hauler

Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how xBeauGaming handles information for Dungeon Hauler.

Contact

Developer: xBeauGaming

Email: xbeaugaming@gmail.com

Information We Collect

Dungeon Hauler is designed to be playable without creating an account. The game stores gameplay progress locally on your device, such as save data, inventory, settings, and progression.

If you send feedback or support email, we receive the information you choose to include in that email, such as your message, email address, and any device details you provide.

The app may check a small public version file on this website to tell you when a newer Google Play build is available. This check does not send your save data, feedback text, or player profile to xBeauGaming.

Ads And Purchases

The current test release does not include an ads SDK or Google Play Billing SDK. Future versions may use Google AdMob or other Google services to show ads. Ad services may collect device identifiers, app interaction data, diagnostics, approximate location, and other information needed to provide, measure, and improve ads.

Future versions may use Google Play Billing for purchases, including a one-time Remove Ads purchase. Purchase processing would be handled by Google Play. xBeauGaming would not receive your full payment card details.

How Information Is Used

  • To save and restore game progress.
  • To respond to support requests and feedback.
  • To provide ads and optional purchases if those features are added in a future version.
  • To understand crashes, bugs, and gameplay issues if diagnostic tools are added.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be processed by service providers used by the app, such as Google Play, Google AdMob, email providers, or hosting providers.

Children

Dungeon Hauler is intended for teens and adults. It is not directed to children under 13.

Your Choices

  • You can play without sending feedback.
  • You can delete local game data by uninstalling the app or clearing app storage.
  • You can contact xBeauGaming at xbeaugaming@gmail.com with privacy questions.

Changes

This policy may be updated as Dungeon Hauler changes. The effective date will be updated when the policy changes.
